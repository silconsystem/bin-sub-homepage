These are the RSS music albums I have created using Podcasting 2.0 XML.
The feeds are added to the Podcast Index.
Like this they are discoverable in Podcasting 2.0 compatible apps like: Podcast Guru.
My idea behind creating a stand-alone webapp for the music releases is that the webapp functions as the booklet that used to come with CD's, Cassette tapes or the sleeves of Vinyl records.
The Podcasting 2.0 apps already have the option to send Satoshis to the artists, but if you can provide fans with a link to the album the artist can share much more control, sell merchandise, share tour dates, etc...
And since the music is on a webpage or a mobile app the artists can add a chat, videos, animation, even create a video game or some crazy interactive experience.

Anything is possible !!!

I hope that people who are better programmers and web designers see what I mean and run with it !!!
I chose the content slider as base, in the early 2000's there were some really nice ones with 3D animations, mirror effects, lightning effects and all those good things. They have gotten a bit boring now, and I understand why. Responsive design and cross-platform compatibility is very important.
Being able to show a working prototype on mobile devices is basically the same as an elevator pitch.
But I am figuring it out, The 'Four Track EP' is my latest version of the RSS content slider idea, and its a big difference from the first version.
