FOUR TRACK EP

Project RSS V4V ALBUM/EP
Role musician, Web Designer and coder
RSS FEED Podcast Index
Visit View RSS feed

This EP concept is an example of what I want to create using the RSS feeds, Podcasting 2.0 and V4V.
Music, Videos and other media are in most cases available for free on the internet and artists can not support themselves with music sales alone anymore.
Using Podcasting apps to spread music and receive donations where the consumer decides what your product is worth to them and ranking by boosts is a system that is much more compatible with the modern web and bypasses the shenanigans of the music industry.

My idea is to create a stand-alone release like a single, album or EP with additional features like bonus content, artwork, liner notes, contact information, your tourdates, links to social media, videos, whatever you can create on a webpage or Mobile App you can create around your music.
This is not limited to music, podcasts can also be distributed with extra content.

The projects on this website are all concepts and still need a professional touch to make them responsive and really integrate the Lighting 'BOOST' payment system.
I chose the content-slider as a base since you can make visually appealing webapps and its perfect for parsing XML, the only limit is the artists creativity and skill using HTML5, CSS3&SCSS, JavaScript and all the tools, languages and server side applications of today's internet.
